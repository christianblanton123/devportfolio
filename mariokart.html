Resume

Mario Kart

This Mario Kart game, complete with an AI kart to race against, place and lap indicator, and, of course, ability to fall offstage on rainbow road was completed by me in a week in C++ as part of a video game programming class. In this class we were given skeleton code and had to implement many features from an existing C++ codebase. In addition to Mario Kart, I coded movement mechanics in a spinoff of the famous 3D parkour game Mirror's Edge.
Solo project
Git, C++, SDL, 3D Math

My Contributions

Programming

  • Implemented vehicle controls (steering, acceleration, deceleration).
  • Moved the camera in 3D space to give impression of speed and increased FOV when going fast.
  • Created waypoints and utilized 3D math to assure that the AI would steer into each waypoint to complete laps.
  • Added ability to fall off the race track and respawn at a recent checkpoint.
  • Integrated audio to play race start, normal laps, and last lap music.

Features

Camera changes on going fast
Fall off racetrack and respawn at checkpoint.
Accurate position indicator. In this example, went from 2nd to 1st place.
