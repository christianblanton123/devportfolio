Resume

Turtle Town

Turtle Town is a 2D PC base-building game where players construct and defend a town on the back of a moving turtle.

Key Features:
Unique mechanics of city-building and tower defense combined with a strong focus on exploration – all on the back of a moving turtle!
Beautifully crafted narrative that encourages deep and meaningful relationships with city’s residents.
Explore a magical forest to gather resources, build and fortify your mobile city and keep citizens happy.

USC Advanced Game Project; team of 25+ people.
Unity, Perforce, Git, C#

My Contributions

Programming

  • Implemented state-based AI for enemies including unique movement, attacking patterns including dashing and retreating, ability to steal resources from player, spawning and despawning functionality.
  • Created enemies as specified by design team with unique targeting and melee/projectile attacks.
  • Coded scripts so enemies would properly play animations according to their current state.
  • Stored enemy data, states, decisions, and actions using scriptable objects.
  • Created enemy spawners utilizing object pooling.
  • Debugged building health/enemy interactions so enemies would properly attack and retreat.
